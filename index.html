Instructions

This is the classic rock, paper, scissors game.

You can choose to play at the best of three rounds or at the best of five rounds.
If you choose the three option, you have to win 2 rounds to beat the computer.
And if you choose the five option, you have to win 3 rounds to beat the computer.
To start the game just click the start button, then you only have to click over your choice.

Good luck and enjoy the game!!!!!!!!!!
